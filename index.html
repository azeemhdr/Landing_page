  • Completely Free:
    Enjoy access to all premium features at no cost—learn, read, and grow with the Quran without any hidden fees or subscriptions.
  • Pro Features for Free:
    All features typically behind paywalls—such as tafseer, videos, quizzes, and Quran planner—are available to you completely free.

Features

Interactive Learning (Videos & Quizzes)

Dive into engaging Quranic videos and test your knowledge through interactive quizzes. This unique feature combines visual learning with fun quizzes, making Quranic study both enjoyable and educational.

Comprehensive Tafseer

Explore detailed by trusted scholars to understand the deeper meanings of Quranic verses, helping you reflect on the message of Allah more profoundly.

Quran Planner Individual

Stay on track with your Quranic journey using a personalized planner for reading or memorizing the Quran. Receive daily reminders and monitor your progress to stay motivated.

Group & Individual Khatm-e-Quran

Join others in completing the Quran together, fostering community and spiritual growth through shared milestones and mutual encouragement.

Read & Listen

Experience a clean, distraction-free Quranic text and listen to high-quality recitations by renowned Qaris. Perfect for both reading and listening anywhere, anytime.

Translations in Multiple Languages

TAccess authentic Quranic translations in Urdu, English, and more regional languages, making the Quran accessible and understandable to a wider audience.
